Data Analysis

Cholangitis Study
This report uses R tidyverse and other libraries to clean and explore a dataset coming from a randomized, double-blinded, placebo-controlled clinical trial of the immunosuppressive drug D-penicillamine at the Mayo Clinic. Major points of exploration are linear and logistic regression of the number of days a patient survives from the point of registration, and the status of a patient at the end of a study, respectively. This is supplemented by multivariate data visualization.
Grade: 78/80
Finding the best fictional NBA player
This report uses base Python, pandas and visualization packages to find the objective best player from a fictional NBA timeline I started when I was 12 through 19 years old with the help of my imagination and NBA2K's MyLeague mode. Using a dataset of 87 notable players' statistics, I take as many factors into acocunt to come up with a ranking of each player.
Kaprekar Routine Counter
Kaprekar's number (6174) is the end-result of a sequence, similar to the hailstone sequence. Read the full documentation in the link above. This function, created in Python, counts the amount of routines needed to reach 6174 in the sequence. The function accounts for all requirements for first value in the sequence.
